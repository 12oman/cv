Kaupapa

I am an artist, educator, and developer at the forefront of Aotearoa's creative and technology sectors. With a decade of teaching excellence, I engage diverse learners in transformative education that celebrates the interconnectedness of te ao Māori, art and emerging technologies.

As an empathic leader, I foster collaborative, high-performing teams and cultivate vibrant communities of practice while fostering relationships with industry stakeholders. With experience designing and delivering curricula from levels 5 to 9, I am committed to enriching education at the nexus of creativity, technology, and the interconnected realms of te ao Māori and contemporary art, empowering the next generation of digital innovators and creative leaders.

I deeply understand the necessity for sustainable creative pathways. My students enjoy meaningful work at companies like RocketLab, Xero, PwC, Nexbe and more.
